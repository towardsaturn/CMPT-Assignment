About

Night Crystals was launched in July, 2013 by Crystal Ma, based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Although a newly opened company, Night Crystals has sold over 100 bottles. To read our customer testimonials, please visit the Testimonials Page.

The Customer
Night Crystals strives to satisfy each one of our customers. If you are unhappy about something, or if you have any concerns, you may e-mail us at nightcrystal(.org). Please include "Night Crystals" in the subject header. We will do our best to reply to you as soon as possible.
